## Emphasis and Formatting

```markdown
*Italic* or _Italic_  
**Bold** or __Bold__  
***Bold and Italic*** or ___Bold and Italic___
```

*Italic* or _Italic_  
**Bold** or __Bold__  
***Bold and Italic*** or ___Bold and Italic___

---

- **Unordered List**:
  
    ```markdown
    * Item 1 or - Item 1
    * Item 2 or - Item 2
    * Item 3 or - Item 3
    ```
    
    * Item 1
    * Item 2
    * Item 3

- **Ordered List**:
  
    ```markdown
    1. Item 1
    2. Item 2
    3. Item 3
    ```
    
    1. Item 1
    2. Item 2
    3. Item 3
